I am a research associate and Ph.D. student in the Efficient Deep Learning Group at the Fraunhofer Heinrich-Hertz-Institute (HHI) and the Technical University of Berlin, working under the supervision of Prof. Wojciech Samek.

My current research focuses on neural codecs like transformer-based general-purpose compressors. I also work on neural network compression methods and the efficient transmission of incremental neural data, e.g., within distributed learning scenarios like federated or split learning. My methods leverage explainable AI (XAI) techniques and concepts of information theory.
As a member of the Moving Picture Experts Group (MPEG), I contributed to the 1st and 2nd editions of the ISO/IEC 15938-17 standard on Neural Network Coding (NNC).
